# Team Contract

## Purpose of this Contract

This contract sets out shared expectations and commitments for how our team will collaborate during the course. It is designed to promote accountability, professionalism, and mutual support as we complete individual preparation, readiness assurance tests, in-class activities, and our course project.

---

## Team Norms and Expectations

### Communication

- All forms of communication will be done through Discord.
- Team members must respond to messages within 1 business day.
- All communication will remain respectful, professional, and constructive.
- All teammate should notify the team about:
  - Missing lectures
  - Missing deadlines
  - Major change in codebase
  - Current progress (regularly)
- All team members shall have an equal opportunity to express their ideas and opinion during project development.

---

## Programming and git convention

- All commits must follow [Conventional Commits Specification](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/en/v1.0.0/).
- Force push is not allowed on the main branch.

### Work quality

- Each team member should put in maximum effort in their work.
- Each Pull Request will be reviewed by at least two team members.

## Decision making

- All team decisions will be made through majority vote.
  - If voting yields nowhere, then the team will discuss to agree on a decision.

---

## Conflict resolution

- Conflicts in teams should be resolved internally within the team.
  - If no compromise is reached, then TA or instructor can be consulted for assistance.

---

## Accountability

- All team members must complete their work by the due date that was agreed upon.

---
